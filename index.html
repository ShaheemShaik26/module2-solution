Chicken

Chicken is one of the most versatile and widely consumed meats worldwide. Popular dishes include Grilled Chicken, Chicken Curry, Fried Chicken, Chicken Alfredo, and Chicken Tikka Masala. It is a great source of lean protein, vitamins B6 & B12, and essential minerals like phosphorus and selenium. Regular consumption of chicken can help muscle growth, weight management, and immune system support.

Beef

Beef is known for its rich flavor and high nutritional value. Some well-loved dishes include Beef Steak, Beef Stroganoff, Beef Tacos, Korean Bulgogi, and Beef Brisket. It provides high-quality protein, iron, zinc, and B vitamins, making it excellent for muscle strength, brain function, and red blood cell production. When consumed in moderation, beef can be part of a balanced diet.

Sushi

Sushi is a delicious Japanese dish that combines vinegared rice with fresh seafood and vegetables. Popular varieties include Nigiri, Sashimi, California Roll, Spicy Tuna Roll, and Rainbow Roll. Sushi is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, protein, antioxidants, and iodine, which promote heart health, brain function, and metabolism. Choosing sushi with fresh ingredients ensures a nutritious and satisfying meal.
